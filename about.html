Our Mission

PromoShields exists to make nightlife safer and more enjoyable. We provide eco-friendly, single-use drink covers that protect against drink spiking—empowering people to enjoy themselves without fear.

Our Story

The idea for PromoShields started with a simple question: “How can we prevent drink spiking without ruining the vibe of a night out?” Founded by a young Kiwi entrepreneur, PromoShields combines safety and style in a way that makes a real difference. What began as a local safety initiative has grown into a movement.

Why We Care

Drink spiking is a global issue that affects thousands of people every year. We're passionate about providing a practical solution that clubs, festivals, and individuals can trust. Every PromoShield used is a step toward a safer world.
