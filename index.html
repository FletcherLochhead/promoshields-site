Why Drink Covers Matter

Drink spiking is a serious issue in New Zealand and worldwide. Research shows that 1 in 4 women and 1 in 12 men in Aotearoa have either experienced or witnessed drink spiking. Many cases go unreported, and the impact on victims can be devastating — from immediate health risks to long-term trauma.

PromoShields provide a simple, effective layer of protection for nightlife, festivals, and community events. By normalising drink covers, we can reduce risk, raise awareness, and help people enjoy themselves with peace of mind.
